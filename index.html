About

My name is Kevin Kim and I am a graduate from the State University of New York at New Paltz with a Bachelor of Arts in Biology with a minor in Computer Science. When I entered University, I aspired to be in the medical field and become a doctor. However, as the years went on I discovered that my true passion was in computer programming. After taking several classes in the subject, I found that coding and the process of learning code was much more fulfilling to me than memorizing a medical textbook. Although I may not have as much experience as my peers that have been studying computer languages since year one, that has done nothing but fuel my internal need and motivation for improvement and honing my skills as efficiently as possible.
